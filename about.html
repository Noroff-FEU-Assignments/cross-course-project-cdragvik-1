About us

RainyDays is an online shop selling mens and womens rain jackets designed for a range of different outdoor avtivities. We target people whose interests are being outdoors, hiking, exploring, skiing, camping, canoeing and so on. The jackets emphasize durability and being suitable for a range of different weather types ensuring customers are comfortable, whatever adventure they go on.
